練習問題

提供ありがと

計算のポイント
  • 因数分解
    • 共通因数でくくる。
    • 複数の文字があるときは最高次数の小さい文字で降べきの順にする。
    • 降べきの順で揃えたあと、カッコ内で因数分解できるときはする。
    • ときにはダブルたすきも必要。
  • 根号を含む式の計算
    • \(\sqrt{a^2}=|a|=\pm a\)
    • 絶対値の中が\(+\)の場合と\(-\)の場合で分けて考える
    • 平方完成や立方完成を使って解く場合もある。
      \(x^3+y^3=(x+y)^3-3xy(x+y)\)
  • 二重根号
    • \(\sqrt{a}+\sqrt{b} = \sqrt{(a+b)+2\sqrt{ab}}\)
    • \(\sqrt{a}-\sqrt{b} = \sqrt{(a+b)-2\sqrt{ab}}\)
    • 上の形にならない場合、整数部分をルートの中に入れて２になるようにする。
    • 式全体に\(\frac{\sqrt{2}}{\sqrt{2}}\)を掛けて上の形にする。etc...
  • 連立不等式
    • それぞれの式の範囲の共通範囲が答えとなる。
    • それぞれの式で計算を行う。
    • \(=\)で３本の式がつながっていても、同様に行う。
  • 絶対値の等式・不等式
    • \(|x|=a\)の場合\(x=\pm a\)
    • \(|x|＜a\)の場合\(-a＜x＜a\)
    • \(|x|＞a\)の場合\(x＜-a,a＜x\)
    • 極力\(||\)の中身が\(+\)の場合と\(-\)の場合で場合分けをして計算する。
    • この際、答えは共通範囲ではなく合わせた範囲である。
    • 解が最初の条件に当てはまらない場合は不適となる。

Manga, Anime, and the World (本文)

　People around the world now know and love
Japanese anime. Some characters are familiar to people
who do not usually read manga or watch anime. One of
the reasons for this success is the adjustments that were
made for viewers overseas. Three of them involve titles,
characters, and content.

Titles
　Manga and anime titles are, of
course, originally in Japanese. Some, like
Jo Jo's Bizarre Adventure, are translated
literally into English. However, this does
not work with all titles. For examnle
Knihts of the Zodiac is originally Seinto
Seiya in Japanese. It describes just the
main character, but in English, the title
was changed to something that relates
to the whole story. This made it more
attractive to viewers in foreign countries.

Characters
　Characters' names are often changed in manga
and anime that are mainly for children. It is hard for
children to remember unfamiliar names. With familiar
names, children can focus on the story. For example, the
character Satoshi in Pokmon becomes Ash in English
The name Ash uses three letters from S-a-t-o-s-h-i.

Content
　Japanese cusoms are sometimes adjusted for
non-Japanese viewers. The adjustments can be small
or large. Consider this example of a small adjustment.
In anime in Japan, characters eat onigiri. In Western
versions, they eat cookies. In manga, they don't change
the pictures. Instead they add short explanations. This
way, foreign readers can understand Japanese customs
and behavior.

　Large adjustment might be a change in the
setting. Consider Kyojin no Hoshi, an anime from the
1970s. In it, the main character Hyuma trains very
hard and becomes a professional baseball player. In
the Indian version. its main character plays cricket, a
popular team sport in india.

　In short, perhaps anime became more popular
because of these adjustments. The language and
customs were adapted a little to fit each culture. Think
about your favorite manga or anime. The original is
probably different.

"Me?"
"Yes, you." said the gull.
"I promise."
"Thank you," the gull said. She soon died. Under her body,
Zorba found the egg.

Every night Zorba kept the egg warm. This was difficult,
but he never gave up.
One morning Zorba opened his eyes. A small white head
was looking in his direction.
"Mom. "
"I'm not your mom. But you're lucky. So that's your name,
Lucky."

Zorba took care of Lucky. When dirty, fat rats and mean
cats attacked her, Zorba protected her.
In time, Lucky became a beautiful gull.

"Lucky," Zorba said. "Now it's time to fly."
"Why do I have to fly?" Lucky said.
"You're a gull."
"Me? A gull?"
"Yes. You're a gull. I'm a cat. Each is different. Each is
good. I love and respect you even though we're different."
"So I must fly?" Lucky asked.
"That's correct. Cats do cat things. Gulls do gull things.
Gulls fly."
"I can't fly without your help. Will you teach me?"
"Of course. I promised your mother. And now I promise you."

Lucky's flying lessons started. She tried to fly many times,
but she always failed. One rainy night, Zorba and Lucky went
to a tower.
"Lucky, all the sky is yours. Open your wings and fly."
"I can't." she cried and pulled away from the edge.
"Yes, you can. You can if you really want."
Lucky got back into position and stepped into the air. She
fell a little at first. Moments later she gained control. Her wings
caught the wind, and she started to fly.
"Zorba, you're right. I can fly!" Lucky said and flew away.
"Yes. You can," Zorba said
to the wide, empty sky.

読みトレ44 Kabuki

My host father took me to a famous Kabuki theater today. I didn't know that the play would last so long. We were there for more than four hours!
The story was about revenge. The Japanese was too difficult for me to understand, so I listened to the English translation.
The action scenes were great. There were lots of swordfights, and the actors wore great make-up. I was surprised that the women's roles were acted by men.
That's just like England during the time of Shakespeare! Those actors were really good. They looked and acted just like women.

How Do You Get to the Mall?
本文

"What would you see if you walked across your city, taking a photo every eight steps? This is what I did for my project Urban Earth
I walked across Mumbai, London, and Mexico City. I walked from one edge of the city to the other, through parks, along quiet streets, along busy streets, through markets, and over bridges.
On TV we only see the most amazing and most terrible things about these cities.
I wanted to show what these places are really like for the people who live there.
I also hope Urban Earth is an adventure others will go on in their own cities. When we live in a big city, there are more places or people around us that we don't know so well.
Although we travel to school or work every day, we don't know how people in other parts of the city live Through this project, I want everyone to explore their city and understand it better by experiencing it for themselves.
There are amazing adventures to go on, right outside our door."

主な公式

\(m\) (kg)は物体の質量、\(v\) (m/s)は速度、\(g\)(m/s\(^2\))は重力加速度、\(h\)(m)は基準点からの高さ。

運動エネルギー\(K\)(J)は

$$K= \frac{1}{2}mv^2$$

重力による位置エネルギー\(U\)(J)は

$$U=mgh$$

と表すことができる。

注意点
  • 単位を揃えてから計算を行う。
  • 基準面の場所をよく考えて位置エネルギーを算出する。
  • 質量など、わからない値があってもそのまま計算を行う。
  • エネルギー保存の法則より、運動エネルギー ＋ 位置エネルギーは移動等の前後で変化しない。
  • 速度がマイナスになることはないが、位置エネルギーや高さは基準面次第でマイナスになることがある。
  • 基準面は自由に変更することができるので、自分で定めて計算を行うと楽に行える。
